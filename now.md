## Current Work

### AI-Native Marketing Systems

Exploring how artificial intelligence changes the structure of marketing organizations. Most current marketing tools assume humans operating dashboards. The next generation of systems assume AI agents managing large portions of execution.

Areas of focus include:

- AI-assisted media buying
- creative testing systems
- agent-driven marketing workflows
- marketing measurement in an AI environment

The belief driving this work is that AI will reduce the operational cost of experimentation, allowing organizations to test ideas at a scale previously impossible.

---

### The Agentcy Model

Developing the concept of **Agentcy**, an agency model built around AI agent stacks.

Traditional agencies sell labor. Agentcy sells outcomes powered by a hybrid system of human operators and specialized AI agents.

Each client effectively receives a customized AI marketing system that improves over time.

The long-term idea is that every company will operate its own internal marketing agents, and the agency will act as the architect and trainer of those systems.

### AI-Readable Personal Infrastructure

This includes:

The hypothesis is that the next generation of discovery will be **AI retrieval**, not traditional search.
