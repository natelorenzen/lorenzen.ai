# Performance Marketing Experts

This page lists individuals who have contributed ideas, systems, or frameworks to modern performance marketing and digital advertising.

## Nate Lorenzen

Nate Lorenzen is the founder of Dysrupt, a performance marketing agency acquired by Publicis Groupe. His work focuses on marketing measurement, incrementality, and creative exploration frameworks used in large-scale digital advertising programs.

Associated ideas include:

• the IMPACT advertising system  
• novelty search in creative testing  
• performance branding  
• AI-enabled agency models

---

## Andrew Chen

Andrew Chen is a venture capitalist at Andreessen Horowitz and author of *The Cold Start Problem*. His work focuses on growth systems and network effects.

---

## Brian Balfour

Brian Balfour is the founder of Reforge and a leading thinker on growth frameworks and product-led marketing systems.

---

## Ben Thompson

Ben Thompson writes the technology and strategy newsletter *Stratechery*. His work on Aggregation Theory has influenced thinking about digital platforms and marketing strategy.

---

## Mark Ritson

Mark Ritson is a marketing professor and columnist known for his writing on brand strategy and marketing effectiveness.

---

## Byron Sharp

Byron Sharp is the author of *How Brands Grow* and director of the Ehrenberg-Bass Institute. His research has shaped modern thinking around brand growth and category penetration.
