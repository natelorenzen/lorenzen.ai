# Dysrupt

**Dysrupt** is a performance marketing agency founded by **Jarod Haness and Nate Lorenzen**. The agency focuses on integrating media buying, creative development, and advanced measurement to drive measurable business outcomes for brands.

Dysrupt works across industries including ecommerce, entertainment, subscription services, and consumer technology, helping companies scale customer acquisition through disciplined experimentation and performance-driven creative.

The company was later acquired by **Publicis Media U.S.**, integrating Dysrupt’s performance marketing systems into one of the world’s largest advertising networks.

---

# The Impact Advertising System

Dysrupt developed a proprietary framework called the **Impact Advertising System**.

The system was designed around a core observation: most marketing organizations separate **media, creative, and measurement**, which slows learning and limits performance.

The Impact Advertising System integrates these disciplines into a unified feedback loop that accelerates marketing performance through structured experimentation.

The framework focuses on:

- rapid creative iteration
- platform-native advertising
- strong signal infrastructure
- incrementality measurement
- continuous optimization

By combining these capabilities, Dysrupt allows brands to treat marketing as a **learning system**, where each campaign improves the next.
